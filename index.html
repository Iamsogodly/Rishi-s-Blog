Places I've Visited

big ben

I visited this in March 2024

While the city of London has many amazing tourist attractions, none are better than the Big Ben. Easily recognizable as a tall clock tower standing proudly over the Thames river, this tower was built in 1859 and was the tallest structure in the world until 1882. The initial bell was too big and broke one day. Then they installed a smaller, 12-inch bell, which also broke but was fixed and still gongs today.

My Favourite Movie

Fighter (2024)
Funny, family-friendly and loaded with twists and turns!

Watched it just 3 days after box office release. Easily the best movie I ever watched. It's also family-friendly and understandable for all ages. It is an enjoyable cinema lasting 2-and-a-half-hours. It also contains several famous actors, such as Hrithik Roshan and Deepika Padukone. The plot is that - A reputed fighter pilot is scolded one day by his CO for not guarding the chopper pilots well during pilot training. He then proves himself by fighting against Pakistan for his nation in revenge of a terrorist attack - and destroys a militant camp. Then, he is blamed again for the loss of 2 of his mates. What happens next?
